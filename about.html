About Circular Management System

The Circular Management System (CMS) is a web-based platform designed to simplify the management and distribution of circulars, notices, and announcements within the Computer Science Department.

This system allows administrators and faculty members to upload important circulars, while students can view, filter, and stay updated with the latest announcements efficiently.

Key Features

  • ✔ Upload and manage circulars
  • ✔ Priority-based notice system
  • ✔ Reminder and alert system
  • ✔ Student and admin login
  • ✔ Easy access to all notices
